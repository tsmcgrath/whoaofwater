This is the full story behind "The Whoa of Water". First, we were trying to come up with a good name for our Salish Sea Voyager boat (from Gig Harbor Boat Works). It's both a tandem rowboat and a sailboat. Somehow "Whoa Boat" popped into my head. I love a pun. Even the bad ones. Then at some point shortly after that, I saw that the subtitle of film Avatar 2 was "The Way of Water", and I thought, "The Whoa of Water"! I'm a rafter, a former white-water canoer, and a wanna-be rower and sailor. Once we learn the ways of salt, sand, currents, and tides. Water has been a big part of our lives and so this suits us well. It's both funny and true. It may only be funny to me, but that's enough.
